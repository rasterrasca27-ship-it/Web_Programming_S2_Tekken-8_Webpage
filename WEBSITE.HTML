Fist Meets Fate

The epic conclusion of the Mishima saga

After defeating his father, Heihachi Mishima, Kazuya continues his conquest for global domination, using the forces of G Corporation to wage war on the world.

Jin is forced to face his fate head on as he is reunited with his long lost mother, and seeks to stop his father Kazuya's reign of terror.

New Game. New Modes.

Get enthralled in Tekken 8 with all new games and mechanics making this Tekken the most robust and exciting Tekken in the series!

Arcade Quest

In the new single-player mode 'Arcade Quest', players can create their own avatar and conquer their rivals in a variety of different arcades. This mode serves as an introduction to the gameplay of TEKKEN 8, allowing players to acquire basic knowledge, learn practical techniques, and enjoy a unique storyline and battles different from regular matches. As the protagonist grows and faces increasingly stronger opponents, so do you!

Tekken Fight Lounge

Enter an immersive lobby where players can interact with one another and access new features and game modes. In the lounge, players can create custom Avatars, communicate with other players via chat and emotes, set up matchmaking, and experience the deep customization and variety of game modes via four distinct areas called Battle Area, Customization Shop, TEKKEN DOJO, and Beach Area.

Super Ghost Battle Mode

In the Tekken Fight Lounge, players can go head-to-head with other players ghost data; AI generated NPCs that learn from the player's play data history! Players can play against AI ghosts with real player-like movement that continues to learn player's game play characteristics and habits with ease!

Tekken Ball

First appearing in Tekken 3, the beloved mini game is back and better than ever! In Tekken Ball, epic battles are "fought" on a beach volleyball court!
